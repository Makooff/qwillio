# Qwillio — Product Context

## Product Purpose
AI voice calling platform. Automates outbound cold calls for French B2B agencies and sales teams. The AI agent (Ashley/Marie) calls prospects, qualifies leads, and books appointments — 24/7, without a human on the phone.

## Users
- **Primary**: Directors and founders of small French agencies (marketing, recruitment, real estate, consulting) — 2 to 15 people
- **Secondary**: Solo sales reps and independent consultants wanting to automate prospecting
- **Scene**: A sales manager in Lyon, Tuesday 15h, MacBook open, looking for a tool to replace cold calling for their team of 5. They're tired of manual outreach. They want results, not demos.

## Register
product

## Brand Voice
- Direct, no BS. French B2B, not startup enthusiasm.
- Confident, not flashy. Results-focused.
- Slightly provocative — "votre concurrent utilise déjà ça"
- No emojis. No "révolutionnaire". No "game-changer".

## Anti-References
- Salesforce (enterprise bloat)
- Any white + teal "saas-clean" aesthetic
- Stock SaaS purple-gradient-on-white (exactly what the old design was)
- Friendly chatbot vibes (this is serious outbound sales)

## Tone
Professional, precise, a little intense. Like Linear or Vercel — tools made by people who care about craft.

## Core Value Props
1. Your team sleeps, the bot calls
2. Only pay for answered calls that matter
3. Set up in 10 minutes, first appointment today
4. TCPA-compliant for France B2B
